Overview

The Students' Symposium on AI & Data Science 2025 is a scholarly initiative jointly organized by Finessefleet Foundation and Sanjivani University. It aims to cultivate a culture of research, critical inquiry, and applied innovation among undergraduate students with a keen interest in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. This symposium provides a distinguished platform for students to showcase their research contributions and project developments, engage in intellectual discourse with domain experts, and gain insights into the latest advancements in AI and Data Science. It is designed to encourage academic excellence and collaborative problem-solving, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation.

Open to participants from diverse academic and technical backgrounds, this event fosters an interdisciplinary approach, promoting constructive dialogue, peer learning, and the exchange of novel ideas. Through this initiative, we aspire to nurture the next generation of AI-driven researchers, innovators, and thought leaders.

Call for Papers and Projects

We invite submissions of original research papers and projects on all aspects of artificial intelligence. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Computer Vision
  • Robotics
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Explainable AI
  • Ethical and Societal Impacts of AI
  • AI Applications
  • Data Science
  • Predictive Analytics
  • AI in Real World
